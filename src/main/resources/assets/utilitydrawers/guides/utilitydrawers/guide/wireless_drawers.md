# Wireless Drawers

Wireless Drawers share their contents across a network — any Wireless Drawer set to the same network sees and contributes to the same shared pool of items or fluids, no matter where in the world it's placed. Two Wireless Drawers placed on opposite ends of your base (or in different dimensions) can act as the same storage slot.

## Networks

Every Wireless Drawer belongs to a network identified by three color values, plus a public/private flag:

- **Public networks** can be joined and accessed by anyone who sets their drawer to the same color combination.
- **Private networks** are tied to the player who created them.

Set a drawer's network by matching its colors to another drawer you want it to share storage with.

## Capacity

Wireless Drawers hold significantly more per slot than a regular drawer of the same slot count — by default, double the capacity of the non-wireless version. This multiplier is configurable by modpack developers.

## Locking and Upgrades

Locking and upgrades work the same as regular drawers, but since the drawer's contents are shared across the whole network, locking or unlocking one Wireless Drawer on a network affects every other drawer on that same network too.
